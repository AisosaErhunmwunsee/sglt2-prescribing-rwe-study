# sglt2-prescribing-rwe-study
Real World Evidence analysis of SGLT2 prescribing variation across Leicester GP practices using NHS prescribing data.


# SGLT2 Prescribing Variation Across Leicester GP Practices

## A Real-World Evidence Study

### Project Overview

This project investigates variation in prescribing of Sodium-Glucose Co-Transporter-2 (SGLT2) inhibitors across Leicester GP practices using publicly available NHS prescribing data between 2021 and 2025.

The study focuses on prescribing trends, practice-level variation, prescribing costs, and broader public health considerations associated with implementation of evidence-based therapies within primary care.

---

# Background

SGLT2 inhibitors such as dapagliflozin and empagliflozin have become increasingly important in the management of type 2 diabetes mellitus because of their demonstrated cardiovascular and renal protective benefits.

Major clinical trials including EMPA-REG OUTCOME and DAPA-CKD demonstrated reductions in cardiovascular mortality, hospitalization for heart failure, and progression of chronic kidney disease among patients receiving SGLT2 inhibitors.

This project applies a Real World Evidence (RWE) approach using NHS prescribing data to evaluate how these therapies are being prescribed across Leicester GP practices.

---

# Aim

To evaluate variation in prescribing of SGLT2 inhibitors across Leicester GP practices using routinely collected NHS prescribing data.

---

# Objectives

* Describe prescribing trends between 2021 and 2025
* Compare prescribing rates across Leicester GP practices
* Evaluate prescribing costs
* Explore public health implications associated with prescribing variation

# Key Findings

* SGLT2 prescribing increased steadily between 2021 and 2025
* Dapagliflozin demonstrated slightly higher prescribing volumes
* Considerable variation existed across Leicester GP practices
* Prescribing costs varied substantially between practices
* Findings suggest variation in implementation of evidence-based therapies within primary care
